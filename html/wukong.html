Black Myth: Wukong

Description

Black Myth: Wukong is an action RPG based on the classic Chinese myth "Journey to the West," developed by Game Science Studio. The game features stunning visuals, fluid combat, and a deep narrative inspired by ancient Chinese folklore. Players take on the role of Sun Wukong, battling fierce enemies and mythical creatures using a variety of powers and abilities, including shape-shifting and magic.
