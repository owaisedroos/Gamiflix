London 2012

Description

London 2012 is the official video game of the 2012 Summer Olympics. Players can compete in a variety of Olympic events, ranging from track and field to swimming, archery, and gymnastics. The game brings the excitement of the Olympics to your home with motion controls and competitive multiplayer options.

Gamiflix Review

Review Liked Disliked
London 2012 is a fun and engaging way to experience the excitement of the Olympics. With a range of events and decent visuals for its time, the game does well in capturing the spirit of the competition. However, the controls can be inconsistent, particularly in events that require precise movements. Multiplayer adds some value, but the lack of depth in some game modes makes it feel repetitive after a while. - Wide range of Olympic events
