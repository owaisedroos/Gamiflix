Valorant

Description

Valorant is a free-to-play first-person tactical hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games. It is a first-person shooter game, consisting of two teams of five, where one team attacks and the other defends. Players control characters known as 'agents', who all have different abilities to use during gameplay.
