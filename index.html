FAQ

What is Gamiflix?

Gamiflix is your trusted source for comprehensive game reviews, top game lists, and genre-specific recommendations.

How are reviews conducted?

Our reviews are conducted by experienced gamers who analyze gameplay, graphics, story, and overall experience.

What are your grades based on?

We rate games by comparing them to others on the same system, aiming for a balance of ">40" and "<70" ratings. ">40" doesnâ€™t mean unplayable but indicates a lower rank. Our main focus is on "fun," with graphics, sound, and control as secondary factors. Reviews are personal, light-hearted, and subject to revision.

When is a game classified as a Top-Game?

Gamiflix designates a game as "Top-Game" when it achieves a score of 80 or higher and has been reviewed by a minimum of 15 publications. The games in the "Top-Game" section are highly acclaimed and have been reviewed by a broad cross-section of the best critics.

Do you write all of these reviews?

We write most of our reviews but occasionally accept RPG reviews from our crew to fill gaps. We edit these contributions to fit the site's format.

Why won't Gamiflix update the review score?

Gamiflix only accepts the first review and score from each publication for a game. This policy, aimed at protecting critics, prevents outside pressure to alter scores, as we clearly communicate to all new publications.
